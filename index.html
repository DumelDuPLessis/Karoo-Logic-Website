Ignite ERP

Built for The Admin People — specialists in the built environment

The Admin People, through experience in the built environment, understood the ERP needs of project owners, professional service providers, and building contractors. Where most ERP systems were designed for regulation compliance, they realised the industry needs a solution designed for efficiency. By improving efficiency, quality improves and regulatory compliance follows naturally.

The Admin People had the know-how and Hugobele had the money. With these two partners, Karoo Logic developed a unique and efficient ERP structure — with basic financial and HR functionality for individuals and organisations, and a comprehensive project document management system for infrastructure development projects.

The document management system was recently launched. Its income is expected to replace further capital investment with dividends, marking the start of a continuous self-funded project that will benefit all three partners continuously.

Without The Admin People's expertise and market infiltration, Karoo Logic's dedicated development at below-cost rates, and some cash from Hugobele, the world would have been stuck with traditional ERP systems for a long time to come.

Our AI Policy

AI is our best friend.
Not our developer.

AI has proven itself as a crucial companion for software developers. On its own, it does not generate optimal code for specific objectives. Developers who allow AI to randomly generate the foundation for a scalable system will be exposed when that system responds inconsistently and becomes unstable.


Since we are in the business of scalable systems, we use humans to write optimal code according to set standards — while leaning strongly on the information, knowledge, and recommendations offered by AI. Our best friend since Google.

🧠

AI for research & knowledge

We use AI as a research accelerator, code reviewer, and knowledge base — not as a code generator for production systems.

⚡

Humans for optimal code

Small or simple systems tolerate average code. Scalable systems do not. Our developers write to strict specifications.

🔒

Stability & reliability at scale

When your system needs to grow, it will. Because the foundation was written right — not randomly generated.
